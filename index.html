About Me

Graduating in Computing and Information Technology over 4 years ago, I have been searching for which road I wish to pursue in life, from landing a job as an IT technician, to Software Support, to then traveling for over a year to places like Montreal, Canada and Oahu, Hawaii, using all my savings just to find what really sparks my interest.

After much thought (and realising that my most enjoyable part of Uni was Web-dev), I have decided to go into this field of front-end development, as it is something I have ALWAYS been interested and I am very excited to see where this leads.

In the picture above I am playing in a Flag Football Tournament in Hawaii where I created some life-long friendships. I pretty much enjoy all kinds of sports however especially interested in sports communities that are diverse and inclusive as that is something I am incredibly passionate about. The Tournament held here was for an LGBTQ+ community and as a member of this community myself, it meant a great deal to me.

If I'm not doing strange american sports, I am usually playing World of Warcraft or watching horror films.
